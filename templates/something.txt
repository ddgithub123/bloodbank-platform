🌿 [Segment 3: Naturopathy – Maharashtra & Puducherry | Team Member 4] (~1.5–2 minutes) Narration:  "Naturopathy is not just a hea system — it’s a lifestyle. Based on the principle that the human body has the inherent ability to heal itself, it focuses on natural elements like water, mud, sun, fresh air, and diet.  Maharashtra played a vital role in spreading Naturopathy in India. Inspired by Mahatma Gandhi’s belief in self-discipline and nature cure, many Naturopathy centers were founded here in the early 20th century. Gandhi himself followed a strict natural lifestyle and believed that true health lay in cleanliness, simplicity, and connection to nature.  Treatments include hydrotherapy using water jets or baths, mud therapy for detox, and fasting or raw diets to reset the digestive system. Yoga and breathing exercises are key components too.  In Puducherry, the coastal town’s peaceful atmosphere makes it an ideal destination for natural healing. Centers like Auroville promote Naturopathy in combination with spiritual healing and community living. Here, health is seen as a balance between the inner and outer environment.  Unlike conventional medicine, Naturopathy does not treat symptoms — it addresses the root cause and aims to empower the body to heal on its own."
